# Breath of Life Ministries
> Catholic Suicide Prevention & Mental Health Ministry

## Crisis Support
- **988** - National Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text, 24/7)
- **741741** - Crisis Text Line (text HELLO)
- **911** - Emergency services

## What We Offer
Breath of Life Ministries provides faith-based mental health support and suicide prevention resources. We serve all people regardless of faith background.
